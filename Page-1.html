Our Products

True Balcony

  True balconies are a part of the building structure that protrudes out and is used to stand outside the roofline. A bolt-on balcony is a structure built independently from the building and attaches to it with standard bolting. When a house is already built, it is too late to construct a standard balcony without significant structural intervention.

 Our bolt-on balcony system allows people to install a balcony with minimal demolition and construction effort.

Faux Balcony

  A faux balcony is an iron balcony with a planter base. If your intention is to use your iron balcony as fall protection and decoration, but not to stand on it, then you need a Faux iron balcony. 

 These Balconies are great for homeowners and businesses that are looking to elevate their project without commiting to a stand-on balcony. Check out our Balcony Accessories for ideas on how to decorate your Faux Balcony.

Guardrail Balcony

  Guardrail Balconies are made without a base, since its purpose is only to safeguard a door that is located on an upper floor. However, they can also be used as a window balcony for extra security.

 Our balconies are 100% residential and Commercial code compliant. Guardail Balconies are attached to the outside of the building with brackets. The number of brackets provided varies depending on the size. All hardware is included and consists of hot deep galvanized lag bolts.
